What is Petey's?

Petey's Pet Parlor is your one stop shop for all your pet needs.

Here at Petey's, we aim to offer customers the finest selection of exotic and house pets. In addition to that, we carry all popular food brands and toys for your happy little friends.

Our employees are pet people first. By that, we don't mean we actually hire cats and dogs, but people who have first hand experience taking care of animals. This way, we know at Petey's, you are getting the best service possible and top notch advice from all of our specialists.

If you are someone you know wants the best for your pets, come stop in to Petey's!
